Description:
The raw datasets for Google Carbon-Free Energy (CFE) percentages and Grid Carbon Intensity were sourced from Google Cloud Sustainability Reports for the years 2019-2023.

Additional Notes:
- The file google_cfe_2019_2023.csv contains the merged, consolidated, and cleaned dataset.
- Intermediate files include preprocessed versions of the raw datasets with:
  - Missing values handled
  - Standardized column names and formats
